Water Whirl on North & South Hemispheres

This is a convincing answer to a myth about how water whirls in different directions depending on whether you happen to be on the North or South Hemisphere.

This myth has been a interesting subject to me for a long time. Besides that, the way these two guys representing their experiments was fasinating. They created two separated videos, one representing what would happen in the northen part of hemisphere, and another for the same test down the south. They edited their video in a way that you can actually enjoy watching both videos at same time, while maintaining a smooth transision back and forth.

However to watch the two videos concurrently you have to sync up two videos manually in order to really enjoy them. To make life easier, I created this page for you. This page should loads both videos and start to play them automatically starting at right time, so that you do not need to manually sync the two videos.
